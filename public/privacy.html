Privacy Policy for DawaLens AI

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

Welcome to DawaLens AI. We are dedicated to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This privacy policy applies to our application hosted at https://noorpos.in and https://dawalens.vercel.app.

1. What Information We Access and How We Use It

DawaLens AI is an AI-powered medication scanner and scheduler designed to assist you in organizing your personal medical reminders.

  • Google OAuth and Google Tasks Integration: If you connect your Google Account, we request access to the Google Tasks API (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/tasks) and basic profile information. This is used strictly to authenticate your account and sync, create, edit, or check off your medication schedules and alarm events as tasks inside your Google account. We do NOT store or compile profiles of your task lists on our servers, nor do we share this data with any third parties.
  • Medicines & Prescriptions Data: Any medicine name, dosage, or scheduling frequency you input or scan is saved securely inside your private cloud database (Firebase).
  • Zero-Knowledge End-to-End Encryption (E2EE): You have the option to enable E2EE. When enabled, your medical inputs are encrypted locally in your browser using the Web Crypto API before being transmitted to the cloud database. We do not hold your decryption keys and cannot read your encrypted records under any circumstances.

2. Google User Data Policy & Limited Use Compliance

Our application strictly complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

DawaLens AI's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We do not transfer, sell, or disclose your Google User data to marketing networks, data brokers, or third-party advertising platforms.
